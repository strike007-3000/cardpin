## Source Attribution

CardPin's data is sourced from publicly available information including official bank websites, card product pages, and publicly accessible terms and conditions documents. All reward rules in CardPin include a `source` object with:

- **sourceUrl**: A link to the public page where the information was found
- **verifiedAt**: The date the information was last checked
- **verifiedBy**: The contributor who verified the information

If you believe any data in CardPin is incorrect, please [open an issue](https://github.com/cardpin/cardpin/issues) or submit a pull request with corrected data and a valid source link.
