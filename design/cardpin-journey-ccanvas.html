CardPin Journey Design Canvas

Comparative view of 3 journey perspectives for optimizing CardPin's user flow: Data-Focused (Detailed math & transparency), Action-Focused (Instant tap & low friction), and Storytelling (Guided narrative & context).

1. Data-Focused Design

Analytical & Formula-First

Stage 1: Context Setting

Dense inputs with immediate fee & FX toggles.

FX Rate: 1 EUR = 1.08 USD (0.5% fee)
Stage 2: Recommendation & Math
Primary Owned Card
Visa Signature Rewards
+€14.75 Net Return

Math: (€420 × 3.5%) - €0.00 FX = €14.70 net

Stage 3: Opportunity Cost & Unlock
Catalog Opportunity: Unlock Mastercard World Elite for an extra +€9.10 on this transaction.
Key Advantages:
  • Complete transparency into net rewards & fees
  • Builds trust for reward power users
  • Seamlessly integrates opportunity cost calculation
2. Action-Focused Design

Instant-Tap & Minimalist

Stage 1: Quick Search

Auto-focused merchant search with quick spend presets (€10, €50, €100).

Stage 2: Instant Tap Advice
TAP THIS CARD NOW
Visa Signature
3.5% Cashback at Delhaize
Stage 3: Wallet Quick Select

Horizontal card carousel showing alternative wallet cards ranked by payout.

Key Advantages:
  • Ideal for fast 2-second in-store decisions
  • Zero cognitive overload for casual spenders
  • Mobile-first touch targets
3. Storytelling Design

Guided Narrative & Insights

Stage 1: Spend Context

Interactive step wizard: "Where are you paying today?"

Stage 2: Narrative Rationale

"Because you spend €420 in foreign currency, your Visa Signature earns €14.75 while avoiding a 2.5% FX fee penalty."

Stage 3: Unlock Journey
Portfolio Growth: You're missing out on €100+/year by not having a zero-FX grocery card.
Key Advantages:
  • Educates users on reward optimization logic
  • Great for onboarding & non-expert users
  • High engagement narrative flow
